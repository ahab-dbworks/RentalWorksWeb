Privacy Policy Scope
This privacy policy applies to the QuikScan app, services, RentalWorks database, and other RentalWorks or TrakitWorks products. The privacy policy addresses the use of your information by Database Works. This privacy policy does not address the use of your information by our customers who purchase the RentalWorks or TrakitWorks system for their businesses.
Client Software
The QuikScan client uses HTML5 local storage and session storage to store information that we use to improve the usability of our application. Local storage provides a place to store information that persists after the application has exited, while Session storage provides a place to store data while the application is running. We use local storage to store some personal information such as your login email address, so we can default your email address on the login screen. We use Session Storage to store some personal information such as your name, so that we can display who is logged in to the application.
Services
QuikScan uses stateless web services to transfer data between the RentalWorks QuikScan client and the RentalWorks Database. Your personal information may be accessed by the web services in order to fulfill web requests made by the client. Any personal information that is stored by the services is stored in the RentalWorks Database.
Database
The RentalWorks and TrakitWorks databases stores personal information about you in your user or contact record and is used for purposes related directly to providing RentalWorks services, such as authentication, security, sending out emails, auditing who made changes to a record, and other functionality needed as part of the RentalWorks system.
How Database Works Uses Your Personal Information
Database Works may have access to your personal information obtained through QuikScan products and services, particularly if we are hosting QuikScan. Database Works does not collect information from our customer's databases, however we do need to use the information at times in order to provide support, system maintenance, design, enhancements, testing, or similar activities necessary to maintain the system for our customers. We consider information in our customer's databases to be private and confidential.
